About This Dashboard

Environmental Justice Index (EJI): CDC's measure combining social vulnerability, environmental burdens, and health vulnerabilities. Higher percentiles indicate greater environmental justice concerns.

Air Quality Index (AQI): EPA's measure of air quality. Values 0-50 are "Good", 51-100 "Moderate", 101-150 "Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups", etc.

Data Sources: CDC Environmental Justice Index 2024 and the EPA Air Quality Data 2024
